Our love story...

Welcome to our love story! It all began at the serene pond at Bass Pro, where fate brought us together. With a cup of tea in hand, we instantly connected over our shared love for adventure and learning. From that moment on, our journey has been a whirlwind of exploration and togetherness. We've traveled to far-off lands, dived into thrilling escapades, and even cruised through life on our motorcycles. Along the way, we've nurtured not only our garden but also the loving bond we share with our cherished pets. Our love blossoms with each passing day, and we can't wait to embark on this new chapter of our lives, hand in hand, forever.


Join us as we tie the knot and celebrate the love that has grown from that beautiful day at the pond. Our wedding will be a reflection of the adventures we've shared and the knowledge we've gained side by side. It's a day where our hearts will be full of love and joy, surrounded by family and friends who have supported us throughout our journey. We invite you to witness our love story and be a part of our future as we promise to continue exploring, learning, and caring for each other and the world around us. Let's make new memories together and celebrate the beginning of forever.
